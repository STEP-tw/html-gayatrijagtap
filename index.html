Beautiful Himachal

Himachal Pradesh is a state in the northern part of India. Situated in the Western Himalayas, itis bordered by states of Jammu and Kashmir on the north, Punjab on the west, Haryana on the southwest, Uttarakhand on the southeast, and Tibet on the east. At its southernmost point, it also touches the state of Uttar Pradesh. The state's name was coined from the Sanskrit: Him means 'snow' and achal means 'land'.

The mountainous state with its Himalayan landscapes attracts tourists from all over the world.
